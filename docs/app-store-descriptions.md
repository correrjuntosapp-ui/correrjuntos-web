STRUCTURED TRAINING PLANS

Seven plans with 322 sessions and 1,025 detailed steps:

• Start Moving (6 weeks) — FREE
• Couch to 5K (8 weeks) — FREE

• Half Marathon (16 weeks)

Every step tells you what to do minute by minute: target pace, heart rate zone, repetitions, rest. Compatible with BLE heart rate monitors.

LOCAL MEETUPS WITH REAL CLUBS

• Beer Runners Málaga — Tuesdays 20:30

ACHIEVEMENTS & COMMUNITY
