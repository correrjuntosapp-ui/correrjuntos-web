Munich's Green Running Paradise

Munich offers runners exceptional green spaces and river trails within an elegant Bavarian city. The massive Englischer Garten rivals Central Park in size, whilst the Isar River Trail provides scenic long-run routes. CorrerJuntos connects you with Munich's organized, outdoor-loving running community that embraces all seasons.

Whether you're training for the Munich Marathon in October, running through Olympiapark's 1972 Olympic facilities, or exploring Nymphenburg Palace's manicured grounds, Munich combines natural beauty with German efficiency. Find your crew and experience Bavarian running culture.

Englischer Garten

Explore one of the world's largest urban parks with over 78km of paths, river-surfers at Eisbachwelle, and endless route combinations through meadows and forests.

Isar River Trail

Run alongside the beautiful alpine-fed Isar River on maintained trails stretching from southern suburbs through the city to northern parklands.

Olympic Park & Palaces

Train at Olympiapark's iconic facilities and hill climbs, or discover the elegant paths around Nymphenburg Palace and its extensive gardens.

Popular Running Areas in Munich

  • Englischer Garten - 78km of paths in one of the world's largest city parks
  • Olympiapark - 1972 Olympic facilities with varied terrain and lake
  • Isar River Trail - Scenic riverside paths through the city
  • Nymphenburg Palace - Royal grounds with formal gardens and parkland

Major Running Events

The Munich Marathon in October showcases the city's beautiful routes past Olympic Park, through Englischer Garten, and along the Isar. parkrun locations grow throughout the city, and clubs like Munich Road Runners welcome international members. Munich's running culture emphasizes outdoor activity year-round with proper gear.
