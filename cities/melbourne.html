Melbourne's Legendary Running Scene

Melbourne is synonymous with running culture in Australia. The famous Tan Track around the Royal Botanic Gardens is where legends train and beginners find their stride. From riverside trails along the Yarra to the scenic Albert Park Lake loop, Melbourne offers runners unmatched variety and a deeply passionate community. CorrerJuntos connects you with this thriving scene.

Whether you're chasing PBs on the Tan, training for the Melbourne Marathon in October, or exploring the Merri Creek Trail on weekends, Melbourne's year-round temperate climate and extensive trail network make it a runner's paradise. Find your group and experience why Melbourne takes running seriously.

The Iconic Tan Track

Run Melbourne's most famous 3.8km loop around the Royal Botanic Gardens. Join hundreds of runners daily on this challenging hilly circuit beloved by Olympians and weekend warriors alike.

River & Trail Running

Explore the Yarra River Trail stretching from the CBD to outer suburbs, or discover the Merri Creek Trail's peaceful green corridor through Melbourne's north.

Lakeside Loops

Train at Albert Park Lake's flat 5km loop with F1 Grand Prix track views, perfect for tempo runs and group training sessions with stunning city skyline backdrops.

Popular Running Areas in Melbourne

  • Tan Track - 3.8km iconic hilly loop, Melbourne's running mecca
  • Yarra River Trail - Extensive riverside paths through the city and suburbs
  • Albert Park Lake - Flat 5km loop with city and lake views
  • Merri Creek Trail - Peaceful 20km+ green corridor through northern suburbs

Major Running Events

The Melbourne Marathon in October is Australia's premier marathon event with stunning bayside and city routes. Run Melbourne offers distances from 5km to half marathon through the CBD. The Tan hosts weekly time trials and club sessions, whilst parkrun locations across Melbourne provide free Saturday morning 5ks. Melbourne Track Club and other elite groups regularly train on the Tan.
