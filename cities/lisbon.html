Lisbon's Hills and Waterfront Running

Lisbon offers runners a unique blend of challenging hills and beautiful waterfront paths. Monsanto Forest provides extensive trail networks within the city, whilst the Belém waterfront and Tagus River paths offer flat, scenic alternatives. CorrerJuntos connects you with Lisbon's growing international running community that embraces the city's varied terrain.

Whether you're training for the Lisbon Marathon in October, exploring the modern Parque das Nações, or discovering the Cascais coastal promenade, Lisbon's mild climate and stunning views make every run memorable. Find your crew and experience Portuguese running culture.

Popular Running Areas in Lisbon

  • Monsanto Forest - 900 hectares of urban forest with extensive trail network
  • Belém Waterfront - Flat riverside path past Jerónimos Monastery and Belém Tower
  • Parque das Nações - Modern waterfront promenade along the Tagus River
  • Cascais Promenade - Scenic coastal route from Carcavelos to Cascais

Major Running Events

The Lisbon Marathon in October offers stunning riverside and coastal routes with perfect autumn weather. parkrun locations are growing throughout the city, and running clubs like Maratona Clube de Portugal organize regular group training. The city's mild climate supports year-round running with international and local participation.
