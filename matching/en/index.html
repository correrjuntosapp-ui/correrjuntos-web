Smart matching across 5 factors

Compatible pace

The algorithm compares your pace ranges (min/km) to make sure you can run together without anyone holding back.

Perfect schedule

Day of the week and time slot matching: morning, midday, afternoon or evening. You only see runners you can actually meet.

Same level

Beginner, intermediate or advanced. Running with someone at your level makes every kilometer more productive and motivating.

Common goals

Training for a half marathon? Looking for social jogs? Matching prioritizes runners with the same goal as you.

Your same city

You only see runners from your city. No matches 200km away that you'll never meet in person.

Active runners

The algorithm values platform activity. Runners who participate more show up higher in your results.
