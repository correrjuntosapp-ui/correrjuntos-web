Berlin's Dynamic Running Scene

Berlin's running culture reflects the city itself: diverse, energetic, and filled with history. From the vast expanse of Tiergarten to the unique open space of former Tempelhof Airport, Berlin offers runners unmatched variety. CorrerJuntos connects you with the city's vibrant international running community.

Whether you're chasing a personal best at the legendary Berlin Marathon or exploring the 160km Mauerweg trail that follows the former Berlin Wall, there's a running group ready to join you. Berlin's flat terrain and extensive green spaces make it a runner's paradise year-round.

Urban Green Spaces

Explore Tiergarten's 5km loop through Berlin's central park, or enjoy the unique experience of running on Tempelhofer Feld's former airport runways.

Historic Routes

Follow the Mauerweg trail along the former Berlin Wall path, combining fitness with powerful historical reflection across 160 kilometers.

Forest Trail Running

Escape to Grunewald's extensive forest trails west of the city, or explore Treptower Park's riverside paths along the Spree.

Popular Running Areas in Berlin

  • Tiergarten - Central 5km loop through historic park with monuments
  • Tempelhofer Feld - Former airport, now 355 hectares of open running space
  • Mauerweg - 160km historic Berlin Wall trail around the city
  • Grunewald - Forest trails, lakes, and hill training opportunities
  • Treptower Park - Scenic riverside running along the Spree

Major Running Events

The Berlin Marathon in September is renowned as one of the world's fastest courses, with multiple world records set on its flat streets. The Berlin Half Marathon in spring attracts thousands of runners. Berlin's parkrun culture is growing rapidly, with multiple locations hosting free Saturday morning 5ks. Nike Running Club and adidas Runners host regular weekly group runs across the city.
