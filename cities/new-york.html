Central Park Running

The 6.1 mile loop is NYC's running mecca. Join groups for early morning repeats, casual weekend loops, or structured training sessions with seasoned marathoners.

Waterfront Routes

Explore the 11-mile Hudson River Greenway, Brooklyn's waterfront parks, or the scenic East River Path with stunning Manhattan skyline views.

NYRR Community

Connect with New York Road Runners members, join weekly group runs, and train for the NYC Marathon with experienced local athletes.

Popular Running Areas in New York

  • Central Park - Iconic 6.1 mile loop with rolling hills and reservoir views
  • Prospect Park - Brooklyn's crown jewel with a 3.35 mile car-free loop
  • Brooklyn Bridge - 1.3 mile iconic crossing with skyline panoramas
  • Hudson River Greenway - 11 miles of waterfront running from Battery to the Bronx
  • East River Path - Brooklyn and Manhattan waterfront routes with bridge views

Major Running Events

The NYC Marathon in November is the world's largest, with 50,000+ runners conquering all five boroughs. The Brooklyn Half Marathon in May showcases the best of Brooklyn, while NYRR hosts weekly races year-round, from 5Ks to half marathons. Central Park Road Runners and Brooklyn Running Company lead group training sessions for all levels.
