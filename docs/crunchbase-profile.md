### Long description (EN, ~1500-2000 chars recommended)

```
CorrerJuntos is a Spanish running app founded in November 2025 in Huelva, Andalusia, by Abraham Márquez Rodríguez — a sub-3:30 marathoner with 8,000+ km in his legs.

WHAT THE APP DOES

CorrerJuntos combines three elements in one mobile product:

(1) An adaptive AI coach (Coach José and Coach Ana) that analyzes every recorded run — pace, cadence, heart rate via BLE chest strap, perceived effort — and rewrites the next workout. It is not a pre-recorded script; the plan responds to actual performance.

(2) Local group meetups powered by real partner running clubs. Currently active across Andalusia, Spain: Beer Runners Málaga (Tuesdays), Sevilla Running Club (Tuesdays + Thursdays), Soul Run Club Huelva (Wednesdays) and Correr Sin Límites Torre del Mar (Thursdays). Any user can join without prior contact with the club.

(3) Seven structured training plans — 322 sessions, 1,025 individual steps — covering Start Moving, Couch to 5K, 5K race, 10K race, Trail Prep, Half Marathon and Marathon.

PRICING

Two complete plans free forever (Start Moving + Couch to 5K). Premium subscription at 4.99 €/month or 29.99 €/year — roughly 3× cheaper than alternatives like Runna and 40 % cheaper than Strava Premium.

TRACTION (May 2026)

- 612 active runners across 8 Spanish cities
- 4 active partner running clubs
- 4.8★ on App Store
- 481 published blog articles (243 Spanish + 238 English)
- Available on iOS and Android

POSITIONING

CorrerJuntos is the first Spanish-first running app combining adaptive AI coaching with real local club partnerships. Voice coaches are recorded natively in Spanish (not translated). Spanish races (101 km de Ronda, Carrera de la Mujer, San Silvestre Vallecana, Maratón de Málaga) are integrated into the plan generator.

Bootstrapped — no external funding to date.
```
